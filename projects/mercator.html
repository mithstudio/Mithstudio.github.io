Wonen in

For the new construction project 'Living in Mercator' (Wonen in Mercator), YUCONVR provided the visuals that bring the plan to life. With an eye for atmosphere, light, and detail, the team showcased what the homes will really feel like — from the architecture to the surrounding green environment. The design embraces natural light, warm textures, and a seamless connection between indoor and outdoor spaces. Each interior has been carefully crafted to reflect a sense of comfort and modern elegance, with open living spaces that flow naturally from one area to the next.
