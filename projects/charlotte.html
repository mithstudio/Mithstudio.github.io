House in
Charlotte

This project is a high-end 3D architectural visualization titled 'House in Charlotte,' created by Michal Nowak and Patryk Kusz from Nowak Studio. The project showcases incredibly photorealistic renderings of both the exterior architecture and the interior design of a modern residence. Set against a backdrop of lush nature and trees, the visualizations highlight the seamless flow between the outdoor environments (patios, landscaping, and structural facade) and the warm, contemporary indoor living spaces. Created using Autodesk 3ds Max, Corona Renderer, Adobe Photoshop, Lightroom, and Creative Cloud, every detail has been meticulously crafted to achieve an unparalleled level of realism.
