The
Arbour

The Arbour was conceived as a serene retreat where architecture quietly blends with nature. A warm, restrained material palette sets the tone, while a sculptural staircase becomes the central symbolic gesture of the home. Large glass facades blur the line between indoors and outdoors, and the common areas transition naturally from one zone to another. Textures, volumes, and sightlines are arranged to maintain balance and visual clarity, with the staircase anchoring the entire composition.
